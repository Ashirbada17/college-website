About Our School

"Swarnalata College of Medical Science, founded in 2015, offers comprehensive medical and nursing education with a focus on academic excellence, skill development, and healthcare service."

Our Vision

To be a premier nursing institution known for academic excellence, clinical expertise, and producing compassionate healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

To provide quality nursing and medical education that blends theory with hands-on practice to develop skilled and ethical caregivers.

Our Excellence

100% graduation record with successful placements in leading hospitals and healthcare institutions across Odisha and beyond.

Academic Programs

We offer a range of professional nursing and healthcare programs designed to equip students with theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and clinical experience. Our curriculum is aligned with national standards and emphasizes hands-on training, ethics, and patient care excellence.
